Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

Mindfulness Research LLC, doing business as Stillful (“Stillful,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use the Stillful mobile application and related services (collectively, the "App").

Stillful is currently offered to users in the United States. Certain privacy choices and defaults may vary based on your location and applicable law. If we expand outside the US, additional disclosures will apply.

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will revise the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, provide additional notice through the App or other means.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide to Us

We collect information when you create an account, sign in, interact with the App, or otherwise communicate with us. This information may include:

  • Account information: email address, display name, authentication credentials, and identifiers provided by third-party sign-in services.
  • Meditation preferences: difficulty settings, selected meditation or breathwork practices, practice goals, and session-related preferences.
  • Privacy choices: your consent preferences for personalized advertising and analytics participation.

B. Information We Collect When You Use the App

When you use the App, we automatically collect information about your usage and device, including:

  • Usage information: meditation sessions completed, practices selected or unlocked, session duration and completion status, difficulty levels, breathwork techniques used, feature usage, and overall progress through the App.
  • Device information: device type, operating system and version, device identifiers, app version, platform (iOS or Android), device locale, country or region derived from locale, and timezone.
  • Technical information: IP address, mobile network information, app performance data, and error logs.
  • Crash and diagnostic information: crash logs, stack traces, device model, operating system version, app state at the time of a crash, and anonymous identifiers. Crash reporting operates regardless of analytics consent preferences, as it is essential to maintaining app stability, security and core functionality.

**Wellness Data Clarification

The App collects information about your meditation preferences, usage, and interactions solely to provide and personalize app functionality. Some users may also choose to share information about how they are feeling when providing feedback or interacting with the App. We do not infer health-related characteristics from this information and process it only for general wellness purposes and consistent with the context in which it was provided. This information is not used to diagnose, treat, or monitor medical conditions. This information is not considered protected health information (PHI) under HIPAA.

C. Information We Collect from Other Sources

We may receive information from third-party services and infrastructure that support the App, including:

  • Authentication providers: if you sign in using a third-party account, we receive basic account information such as email address, display name, and a unique identifier, in accordance with your settings with those providers.
  • Cloud synchronization services: if you are signed in, your meditation progress and preferences may be synchronized across devices using cloud-based infrastructure.

2. How We Use Information

Subject to your consent choices and applicable law, we use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App, including authentication, content delivery, progress tracking, and feature functionality.
  • Synchronize your meditation progress and preferences across devices when you are signed in.
  • Improve the App, including developing new features, analyzing usage patterns, and enhancing performance and reliability.
  • Maintain app stability and security, including crash reporting, diagnostics, fraud prevention, and enforcement of our policies.
  • Communicate with you about your account, transactions, app updates, and features.
  • Send promotional messages about new features or offers where permitted by law, and where you may opt out at any time.
  • Measure and improve advertising effectiveness and display advertisements, including personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your consent choices.
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect the rights, property, and safety of Stillful, our users, and others.

We do not sell personal information for monetary consideration, however, some data sharing may be considered a ‘sale’ or ‘sharing’ under applicable state laws, as described below.

3. Disclosure of Information

We may disclose information about you:

  • With service providers that perform services on our behalf, such as cloud infrastructure, authentication, analytics, advertising, payment processing, and crash reporting. Our service providers process information on our behalf and pursuant to contractual obligations that restrict their use of personal information.
  • For advertising and analytics purposes with partners that help us understand how the App is used and deliver advertisements, subject to your consent choices.
  • With third-party sign-in providers when you choose to authenticate using those services, in accordance with their terms and your settings.
  • For legal reasons if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of Stillful, our users, or others.
  • In connection with business transfers, such as a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets.
  • With your consent or at your direction.

We may also share aggregated or anonymized information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you.

4. Advertising and Analytics

Universal Opt-Out Signals: Where required by applicable law, we recognize Global Privacy Control (GPC) and similar universal opt-out signals as valid requests to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information for targeted advertising purposes to the extent such signals are technically available to us in a mobile app environment. Because the App is a native mobile application, such signals may be limited to the specific device or context in which they are received.

We work with third-party advertising and analytics services to help us understand how the App is used and to display advertisements.

These services may collect information such as advertising identifiers, IP address and approximate location, device and operating system information, ad interaction data, and app usage metrics. Video advertisements may also collect playback and interaction events, such as when a video ad is loaded, viewed, completed, or skipped.

  • Analytics: when analytics is enabled, we collect usage metrics such as session start times, durations, completion rates, practice selections, feature usage, and error events. You can control analytics participation through in-app privacy settings.
  • Advertising: the App may display rewarded video or other advertisements. You may choose whether to receive personalized or non-personalized ads. Declining personalized ads does not reduce the number of ads shown.

Regional defaults for analytics and advertising may vary based on applicable law.

5. Your Choices

Privacy Settings

You can manage your privacy preferences within the App, including:

  • Personalized advertising preferences
  • Analytics participation
  • Push notification permissions

Account Information

You may update your account information through the App or by contacting us.

Delete Your Account

You may delete your account at any time through the App settings or by contacting us. When you delete your account, your authentication data and synchronized meditation progress will be deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Device Controls

You may also control certain data collection through your device settings, such as limiting ad tracking or disabling notifications.

Push Notifications

With your consent, we may send push notifications about meditation reminders, new content, or app updates. You can enable or disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the App.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the App, maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When you delete your account, we delete or anonymize your personal information within reasonable timeframes, subject to legal requirements. Retention periods vary based on the type of information and purpose of processing.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information consistent with industry-standard practices appropriate to the nature of the data. However, no method of transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

9. Data Transfers

Stillful is based in the United States. If you access the App from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States and other countries where our service providers operate, which may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction.

10. Privacy Rights

Sensitive Personal Information: We do not collect "sensitive personal information" as defined by applicable state privacy laws, such as precise geolocation data, government-issued identification numbers, financial account credentials, or information used to diagnose or treat medical conditions. Meditation preferences, usage patterns, and wellness feedback are processed only for general wellness and App functionality purposes.

This section describes privacy rights available to residents of California and other U.S. states with comprehensive privacy laws, including Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, and Utah.

California and Other State Privacy Rights

Sale and Sharing of Personal Information: Under California and other state privacy laws, allowing advertising partners to collect information through the App (such as through advertising identifiers or similar technologies) may be considered a "sale" or "sharing" of personal information, even though we do not receive monetary payment for this data. You may opt out of this sale or sharing for targeted advertising purposes through the App’s privacy settings or as otherwise described in this section.

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA"), as amended by the CPRA, including the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it is used
  • Request access to or deletion of your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information for targeted advertising
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Identifiers (such as email address, device identifiers, IP address, and user IDs)
  • Commercial information (such as purchase or subscription information)
  • Internet or network activity information (such as app usage, session data, and ad interactions)
  • Approximate location information
  • Inferences drawn from app usage to improve user experience

You may exercise your privacy rights by contacting us at stillful.app@outlook.com. We will respond to verified requests within the timeframes required by law.

Verification: To protect your privacy, we will verify your identity before processing requests to access, delete, or correct your personal information. We may ask you to provide information that matches our records, such as your email address or account details.

Authorized Agents: You may designate an authorized agent to submit privacy requests on your behalf. The authorized agent must provide proof of authorization, and we may still require you to verify your identity directly with us.

Appeals: If we deny your privacy request, you may appeal our decision by contacting us at stillful.app@outlook.com with "Privacy Appeal" in the subject line. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law. If you are not satisfied with the outcome of an appeal, you may have the right to contact your state attorney general or regulator.
