Account & Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your Stillful account and associated data at any time.

How to request deletion

  • Email us at: support@stillful.app
  • Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include the email address used in the app (or your user ID, if anonymous)

What will be deleted

  • "Account identifiers"
  • "Usage data linked to your account"
  • "Preferences and settings"

Retention

  • "Anonymous, aggregated analytics may be retained"
  • "Data required for legal or accounting purposes may be kept as required by law"

Timeline

  • "Requests are processed within 30 days"
